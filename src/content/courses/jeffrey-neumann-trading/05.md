# บทที่ 5: Corner 3 - Sector Focus

## Philosophy

> "Don't pick stocks. Pick sectors. Then buy the basket."

Neumann เป็น **Thematic Trader** - ไม่ได้เลือกหุ้นเดี่ยวๆ

## Sector Catalysts

### ประเภทของ Sector Moves

| Type | Example | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| Legislative | Ethanol mandate | Weeks-Months |
| Technological | 3D printing, AI | Months-Years |
| Pandemic | Vaccines, PPE | Weeks-Months |
| Commodity | Lithium, Rare earth | Months-Years |

## The Shotgun Approach

### Concept

```
Hot Sector Identified
        ↓
Buy 5-10 stocks in sector
        ↓
Let winners run, cut losers
        ↓
Pyramid into "Lead Dog"
```

### ทำไมต้อง Basket?

1. **Sector exposure** - ไม่พลาดกระแส
2. **Company risk mitigation** - บางตัวอาจ fail
3. **Identify the winner** - ให้ market บอกว่าตัวไหนแรง

## Lead Dog Strategy

### วิธีหา Lead Dog

```
Basket of 5-10 stocks
        ↓
Watch which one reacts most aggressively
        ↓
That's the Lead Dog
        ↓
Pyramid into it
```

### Characteristics of Lead Dog

- Highest volume surge
- Breaks out first
- Holds gains best
- Most media attention

## Sector Rotation Awareness

### Sector Lifecycle

```
1. Stealth Phase   → Smart money enters
2. Awareness Phase → Early adopters
3. Mania Phase     → Mass public (EXIT HERE)
4. Blow-off Phase  → Crash
```

### Neumann's Exit Signal

> "Golf Course Indicator"

ถ้าได้ยินคนทั่วไปพูดถึง sector นั้น = เข้าสู่ Mania Phase = **Exit**

## Sector Screening

### สิ่งที่มองหา:

- [ ] Sector with recent news/catalyst
- [ ] Multiple stocks breaking out simultaneously
- [ ] Volume flowing into sector ETFs
- [ ] Media coverage increasing

### Tools

- Sector ETF volume
- News scanners
- Social sentiment (as contrarian)

| Stock | Move |
|-------|------|
| PEIX | 1,000%+ |

**Catalyst:** Congressional bill requiring 400% increase in ethanol blending

## Sector Checklist

- [ ] มี macro tailwind ชัดเจน
- [ ] Multiple stocks showing basing patterns
- [ ] Volume flowing into sector
- [ ] ยังไม่ถึง Mania Phase
- [ ] มี catalyst ที่สามารถกำหนดเวลาได้
