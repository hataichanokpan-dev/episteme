# บทที่ 10: Psychology & Daily Routine

## Core Psychology

### High Conviction, Low Frequency

> "I don't play the market. I wait for specific alignment."

| Concept | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| High Conviction | มั่นใจจากการ research |
| Low Frequency | รอ setup ที่ perfect |
| Patience | ไม่ force trades |

### The Anti-Gambler Mentality

Trading = **Professional investigative business**

ไม่ใช่:
- Gambling
- Guessing
- Following tips
- Acting on emotion

### Psychological Neutrality

**Goal:** Maintain "steady pulse" regardless of P&L

| Method | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Meditation | Clear mind |
| Breathwork | Calm nervous system |
| Journaling | Process emotions |
| Exercise | Release stress |

## Daily Routine

### Morning (04:00 - 09:30)

```
04:00 - 05:00  Wake up + Global news review
               ├── Overnight market developments
               ├── SEC filings (Form 4s, S-1s)
               └── Sector news

Pre-market      Review "Universe of Signs"
               ├── Watchlist of potential setups
               ├── Stocks showing volume spikes
               └── Charts to monitor
```

### Market Hours (09:30 - 16:00)

```
Focus on: Price Action
         ├── Is breakout holding?
         ├── Volume confirming?
         └── Any thesis changes?

NOT day trading - Looking for multi-week/month moves
```

### Post-Market (16:00 - 19:00)

```
The Deep Dive (Four Corners Research)
├── Chart analysis
├── Fundamental research
├── SEC filings review
├── Product testing (if applicable)
└── Store visits (if applicable)
```

### Evening (19:00 - 22:00)

```
Reset
├── Meditation
├── Journaling
├── Review day's decisions
└── Prepare for tomorrow
```

## The Universe of Signs

### What It Is

A watchlist of stocks showing **"signs of life"** but haven't broken out yet

### Criteria to Add

- [ ] Volume spike detected
- [ ] Coming off 52-week low
- [ ] Forming base pattern
- [ ] Potential catalyst identified

### Monitoring

```
Daily Review:
- Any new volume spikes?
- Any breakouts occurring?
- Any news/catalysts?
- Thesis still valid for existing positions?
```

## Mental Framework

### Pre-Trade Checklist

```
□ Have I done the research?
□ Do I understand the product?
□ Have I read the SEC filings?
□ Is the setup perfect?
□ Can I explain the thesis in 1 sentence?
□ Do I have an exit plan?
```

### During Trade

```
□ Is thesis still valid?
□ Is price action confirming?
□ Any news that changes the story?
□ Am I emotional?
□ Should I add or reduce?
```

### Post-Trade

```
□ What did I do right?
□ What did I do wrong?
□ What did I learn?
□ How can I improve?
```

## Emotional Pitfalls

### FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

**Symptoms:**
- Chasing stocks already up 500%
- Entering without full setup
- Ignoring red flags

**Cure:**
- Stick to checklist
- Remember: Another train will come
- 100% cash reset if needed

### Revenge Trading

**Symptoms:**
- Trading larger after loss
- Ignoring setup quality
- Wanting to "make it back"

**Cure:**
- 100% cash immediately
- Take break
- Return fresh

### Overconfidence

**Symptoms:**
- Increasing position size
- Skipping research
- Ignoring risk management

**Cure:**
- Review past losses
- Stick to process
- Remember: Market doesn't care

## The 5 Never-Violate Rules

### 1. Never Average Down

```
Price down = Thesis wrong or Timing wrong
Either way = Don't add
```

### 2. Avoid the Crowd

```
Everyone talking about it = Easy money made
Find the next one instead
```

### 3. No Hard Stops

```
Use mental stops based on thesis
Let price action guide you
```

### 4. Know the Filings

```
SEC filings = Your edge
Most traders too lazy = Your advantage
```

### 5. Wait for the Turn

```
Don't catch falling knife
Wait for volume + basing + catalyst
```

## The Ultimate Differentiator

### Boots on the Ground

> "While other traders look at screens, Neumann looks at the world."

**This is the edge:**

| Others | Neumann |
|--------|---------|
| Screen time | Real world research |
| Other people's analysis | Own investigation |
| Fast decisions | Deep understanding |
| Price-focused | Product-focused |

### Psychological Benefit

When you've **tested the product yourself**:
- Conviction is unshakeable
- Can hold through volatility
- Know when thesis changes
- Exit with confidence

## Summary

### The Neumann Method

```
1. Wait for perfect setup (4 Corners)
2. Research deeply (Boots on Ground)
3. Start small (Pilot position)
4. Add when right (Pyramiding)
5. Exit when thesis breaks (Mental stops)
6. Reset when needed (100% cash)
7. Repeat with discipline
```

### Key Takeaways

- **Conviction comes from research**
- **Patience beats action**
- **World beats screens**
- **Process beats prediction**
- **Mental stops beat hard stops**
