# บทที่ 7: Story Evaluation Framework

## Philosophy

> "A stock needs a narrative that can capture the imagination of the mass public."

The "Story" = เหตุผลที่ทำให้คนอยากซื้อ

## Compelling vs Weak Story

### Compelling Story (High Conviction)

| Feature | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **Blue Sky Potential** | สามารถ disrupt industry ทั้งหมด |
| **Simplicity** | อธิบายได้ใน 1 ประโยค |
| **Validation** | Verify ได้ด้วย "boots on the ground" |
| **Under-the-Radar** | คนทั่วไปยังไม่รู้จัก |
| **Technical Setup** | Breaking out of multi-year base |

### Weak Story (Low Conviction)

| Feature | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **Niche** | Market จำกัด |
| **Complex** | ต้องอธิบายยาว |
| **Rumor-Based** | ไม่มี evidence |
| **Already Hyped** | ทุกคนพูดถึงแล้ว |
| **Chasing** | ขึ้นไปแล้ว 500% |

## ตัวอย่าง Compelling Stories

### "This company prints human organs"

```
Story: 3D bioprinting - print human tissue/organs
Simplicity: ✅ 1 sentence
Blue Sky: ✅ Disrupt entire medical industry
Validation: ✅ Can research the technology
Status: Under-the-radar (at the time)
```

**Result:** Organovo (ONVO) $3.50 → $12

### "Ethanol will replace oil"

```
Story: Government mandating 400% increase in ethanol
Simplicity: ✅ Clear legislative catalyst
Blue Sky: ✅ Entire energy sector shift
Validation: ✅ Bill text available
Status: Before mainstream awareness
```

**Result:** Pacific Ethanol 1,000%+ in 10 days

## ตัวอย่าง Weak Stories

### "This company has a new CRM feature"

```
Story: Incremental software improvement
Blue Sky: ❌ Niche improvement
Simplicity: ❌ Technical, boring
Market: Already saturated
```

### "My friend told me this will moon"

```
Story: Tip from friend
Validation: ❌ No verification
Source: ❌ Unreliable
```

## The One-Sentence Test

> ถ้าอธิบาย story ไม่ได้ใน 1 ประโยค = ซับซ้อนเกินไป

### Good Examples

- "This company prints human ears"
- "Government requires 400% more ethanol"
- "This AI can write code"

### Bad Examples

- "This company has a new synergy with their subsidiary that..."
- "The technical indicators suggest a reversal pattern..."

## Boots on the Ground Validation

### วิธี Verify Story

| Story Type | Validation Method |
|------------|-------------------|
| Consumer Product | ซื้อมาลอง, ถาม store manager |
| B2B Product | ถามลูกค้า, ดู reviews |
| Technology | Research papers, patents |
| Regulatory | อ่าน bill text, FDA filings |
| Financial | SEC filings, insider buying |

### ตัวอย่าง: CBD Beverage Theme

Neumann's validation process:
1. ไป convenience stores ในพื้นที่
2. ถาม store managers ว่าขายดีไหม
3. สังเกต customer behavior
4. ถ้าเห็นคนซื้อจริง = Story valid

## Story Lifecycle Awareness

### 4 Phases

```
1. Stealth    → Smart money accumulating
2. Awareness  → Early adopters entering
3. Mania      → Mass public FOMO
4. Blow-off   → Crash
```

### Where to Enter/Exit

| Phase | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Stealth | **ENTER** |
| Awareness | **ADD** |
| Mania | **EXIT** |
| Blow-off | AVOID |

### Warning Signs of Mania

- Mainstream media coverage
- "Golf course conversations"
- Your taxi driver mentions it
- Social media exploding

## Story Checklist

- [ ] อธิบายได้ใน 1 ประโยค?
- [ ] มี Blue Sky potential?
- [ ] Verify ได้ด้วย research?
- [ ] ยังไม่ถึง Mania phase?
- [ ] Technical setup พร้อม?
