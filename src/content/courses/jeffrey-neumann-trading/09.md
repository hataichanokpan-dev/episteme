# บทที่ 9: Exit & Risk Management

## Philosophy

> "The exit is more important than the entry."

Neumann มี exit strategy ที่ชัดเจน 4 แบบ

## 4 Exit Strategies

### 1. Climax Top (Parabolic)

**Signal:** ราคาวิ่งแนวตั้ง

```
Price
  │
  │                           ██
  │                         ██
  │                       ██
  │                     ██
  │                   ██
  │                 ██
  │               ██
  │           ████
  │       ████
  │   ████
  │ ████
  └──────────────────────────────► Time
              SELL HERE ▲
```

**Indicators:**
- Vertical price move
- Record volume
- RSI > 80-90
- Media frenzy

### 2. Story Break (Thesis Invalid)

**Signal:** เหตุผลที่ซื้อเปลี่ยน

| Original Thesis | Thesis Break |
|-----------------|--------------|
| FDA approval expected | Approval denied |
| Product launch Q3 | Delayed indefinitely |
| Legislative catalyst | Bill voted down |
| Sector growth | Sector slowdown |

> "If the reason you bought is no longer true, exit immediately - regardless of price."

### 3. Moving Average Violation

**Signal:** ราคาตัด 50-day SMA

```
Price
  │
  │
  │    ╱╲    ╱╲
  │   ╱  ╲  ╱  ╲
  │  ╱    ╲╱    ╲     ╱
  │ ╱            ╲   ╱
  │╱              ╲ ╱
  │                ╲ ◄── Break 50 SMA = EXIT
  │─────────────────╳─────── 50 SMA
  │
  └────────────────────────────► Time
```

### 4. Time Stop

**Signal:** ราคาไม่ขยับตามที่คาด

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Expected move in 2 weeks | ไม่ขยับ = Exit |
| Catalyst passed | ไม่มี reaction = Exit |
| Opportunity cost | มีโอกาสอื่นดีกว่า = Exit |

## Risk Management

### Mental Stops vs Hard Stops

| | Mental Stops | Hard Stops |
|---|-------------|------------|
| Mechanism | Exit when thesis fails | Auto-sell at price level |
| Pros | Flexibility, avoid stop hunts | Discipline guaranteed |
| Cons | Requires discipline | Can be exploited by MMs |

### Neumann's Choice

> "Hard stops are poverty traps that market makers exploit."

ใช้ **Mental Stops** based on:
- Thesis validity
- Price action
- Moving average breaks

### Position-Level Risk

```
Initial Risk = Position Size × Stop Distance

Example:
- Position: $10,000
- Stop: 20% below entry
- Max Risk: $2,000 (2% of $100K account)
```

### Account-Level Rules

| Metric | Guideline |
|--------|-----------|
| Max positions | 3-5 |
| Max per position | 30%+ (if high conviction) |
| Max total risk | 6-10% of account |

## Handling Drawdowns

### The 100% Cash Reset

> "Being out of the market clears the mental fog of a losing streak."

**When to go 100% cash:**
- Consecutive losses
- Emotional trading
- Forcing setups
- Not seeing clearly

### Benefits of Cash Reset

1. **Psychological clarity** - หาย stress
2. **Fresh perspective** - มองเห็น setup ใหม่
3. **Patience restored** - รอได้นานขึ้น
4. **No baggage** - ไม่มี position มากวนใจ

## Loss Philosophy

### The "Tax" Concept

> "Losses are the cost of doing business."

| Business | Cost |
|----------|------|
| Restaurant | Food spoilage |
| Retail | Inventory shrink |
| Trading | Losing trades |

### How Neumann Handles Losses

1. **Cut immediately** - ไม่รอ
2. **Don't dwell** - ไม่เสียใจนาน
3. **Learn** - บันทึก lesson
4. **Move on** - หา setup ใหม่

## Exit Checklist

### Before Exiting

- [ ] Thesis still valid?
- [ ] Price action changed?
- [ ] Moving average broken?
- [ ] Catalyst passed?

### When Exiting

- [ ] Scale out or all at once?
- [ ] Market conditions?
- [ ] Tax implications?

### After Exiting

- [ ] Document the trade
- [ ] Review what went right/wrong
- [ ] Update lessons learned

## ตัวอย่าง Exit Scenarios

### Successful Exit: Climax Top

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Entry | $2.00 |
| Peak | $12.00 |
| Signal | Parabolic, RSI 90 |
| Exit | $11.50 |
| Return | 475% |

### Failed Exit: Story Break (Inovio)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Entry | Based on Swine Flu |
| Problem | Pandemic scare subsided |
| Mistake | Didn't exit when thesis changed |
| Result | Lost 30% of account gains |

**Lesson:** When story changes, exit immediately
